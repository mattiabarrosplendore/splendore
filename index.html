I — Introduction

Mattia
Barro

Sound Designer  ·  Artist  ·  Journalist  ·  Milan

Fifteen years across indie bands, club music, gallery installations, and cultural journalism. The thread connecting all of it: sound as a way of thinking, not just making.

Milan-based, working internationally. He works at the intersection of experimental music, institutional collaboration, and commercial sonic identity — from gallery commissions to brand campaigns, the same rigour applies: sound as a critical, deliberate act, never decoration.
